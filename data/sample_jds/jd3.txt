ob Title: Data Analyst
Location: [Company HQ or Remote] Department: Business Intelligence / Analytics Employment Type: Full-time
Qualification : B.tech

🧠 Job Summary
We are looking for a detail-oriented Data Analyst to collect, process, and analyze data to help drive strategic decisions. You’ll work closely with cross-functional teams to uncover insights, build dashboards, and support data-driven initiatives across the organization.

🛠️ Key Responsibilities
Collect and clean data from multiple sources (databases, APIs, spreadsheets)

Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies

Build dashboards and reports using tools like Tableau, Power BI, or Excel

Collaborate with marketing, finance, and product teams to understand data needs

Create scripts to automate data collection and transformation

Interpret results using statistical techniques and provide actionable recommendations

Maintain and optimize data pipelines and storage systems

Ensure data integrity, privacy, and compliance with internal policies

🎓 Required Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related field

1–3 years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence

Proficiency in SQL and Python (pandas, NumPy, matplotlib)

Familiarity with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI)

Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

Excellent communication and presentation abilities

🌟 Preferred Skills
Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)

Knowledge of statistical modeling or machine learning basics

Understanding of business KPIs and financial metrics

Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects
